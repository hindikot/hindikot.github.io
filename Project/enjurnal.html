"News of Geophysics in Azerbaijan" magazine

The scientific-technical and information magazine "Geophysics Innovations in Azerbaijan" founded by the Azerbaijan National Geophysics Committee has been published since 1997. Over the years, dozens of articles and articles dedicated to the results of geophysical and geological research of our Republic and foreign scientists and specialists have been published in the magazine, and the magazine has gained international reputation. As a result of the high professionalism, demandingness, hard work and effort of the editorial staff, an interesting magazine in the field of petroleum geophysics has been created. After the table of contents, readers are presented with a brief summary of the articles published in the journal. The publication of the articles and summaries published in the magazine in three languages ​​- Azerbaijani, Russian and English, expands its scope and readership, and makes it possible to be known in the oil-producing countries of the world. The journal also publishes interesting events in the oil industry, informative articles, articles on the history of geophysics, the lives of famous geophysicists and geologists, and anniversary greetings.

Attention authors!

Geophysics, geology, engineering geology, etc. are published in the journal "Geophysics Innovations in Azerbaijan". results of theoretical and experimental works, summaries, informative and discursive articles not published in other publications in the fields are published. Articles must be signed by the authors. A letter addressed to the editor-in-chief, expert report, author's surname, first name, patronymic, place of work, address, telephone and e-mail should be attached to the article. Articles can be submitted by e-mail in three languages ​​(Azerbaijani, English and Russian). The text of the article is typed in the MS Word text editor with Times New Roman 12 font, 1.5 spacing, margins (2.5 cm from the left, 2 cm from the top, bottom, and right). The optimal size of the article should not exceed 10-12 printed pages in A4 format (including text, keywords, tables, graphs, pictures, captions, as well as summaries in Azerbaijani, English and Russian). Pictures and tables are accepted as separate files in *.TIF or *.JPEG format (with a printed copy attached) with a quality of not less than 300 dpi. Materials submitted in PDF format (text, tables and graphics) are not accepted. The maximum size of the photos is A4, and the number should not exceed 7. In raster images, all contours and graphics, as well as numbers and inscriptions, must be written clearly. Mathematical equations should be given in a clear and precise font. In the text, all literature is cited in square brackets (eg, [3]) in order of rank. In the literature, the surname and initials of the authors, the name of the book or article, if it is from a collection - the name of the collection, publisher, year of publication, if it is a book - the number of pages, if it is a magazine article - the name of the magazine, the first and last pages of the article (for example, P. 6- 12). A summary in Azerbaijani, English, and Russian languages ​​is added to the article, each not exceeding 1 page. Duplicate publication of previously published articles and references to unpublished material are not permitted. Articles that do not meet the specified requirements will not be accepted. All articles are sent for review.
